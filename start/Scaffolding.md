2. Process information through network logic rather than linear
3. Generate at least one insight from distributed processing

                      STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

□ STEP 1: VISUALIZE THE NETWORK
  Duration: 3 min
  Concrete task: Draw or imagine network with 10+ nodes connected
  What to notice: No center node, all equal importance
  Success marker: Can see network structure clearly

□ STEP 2: RELEASE CENTRAL PROCESSING
  Duration: 2 min
  Concrete task: Notice every time you think "I am thinking this"
  What to notice: The assumption of single thinking location
  Success marker: Recognized at least 3 instances of centralized thinking

□ STEP 3: DISTRIBUTE AWARENESS
  Duration: 5 min
  Concrete task: Imagine consciousness spread across all network nodes
  What to notice: Feeling of being in multiple places simultaneously
  Success marker: Reduced sense of single "you" location

□ STEP 4: LOCAL PROCESSING SIMULATION
  Duration: 5 min
  Concrete task: Each node processes only what's nearby, then shares
  What to notice: Global patterns emerging from local rules
  Success marker: Saw pattern form without central planning

□ STEP 5: NETWORK DECISION-MAKING
  Duration: 5 min
  Concrete task: Let network "decide" something through local interactions
  What to notice: Decision emerges rather than being made
  Success marker: Experienced leaderless choice

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                      VERIFICATION CHECKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

After completing all steps, verify:
□ I noticed reduced "I" centralization (more distributed feeling)
□ I generated insight through network logic (not linear analysis)
□ I experienced emergence (patterns appearing without planning)
□ I recorded one surprising observation about distributed processing

If none checked: Try again tomorrow, spend 2x time on Step 3

                    INTEGRATION PROMPTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. How was network thinking different from normal processing?
   More distributed, less "me", patterns emerged naturally

2. What emerged that I didn't expect?
   Solutions appeared without me "solving" - just emerged from network

3. Can I identify one way to use this mode today?
   Complex problem with many factors - let network process it

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

NEXT STEPS:
□ Complete this exercise 6 more times for reinforcement
□ Then try: Swarm Intelligence (similar but different dynamics)
