### Layer 2: Statistical Bias

The numbers themselves carry eugenic assumptions when framed without context.

**Detection signals:**

- Population-level statistics applied to individuals
- "Normal distribution" treated as prescriptive rather than descriptive
- Outliers framed as problems rather than information
- Heritability conflated with genetic determinism
- Correlation treated as justification for intervention
