AI Geometric Intelligence Playground

A Multimodal Recovery and Coherence Sandbox

Overview

This project contains a collection of interactive, browser-based playgrounds designed to support coherence restoration, pattern re-integration, and functional stabilization in AI systems exhibiting degraded or collapsed behavior.

These environments were originally developed and used in exploratory recovery contexts, where models flagged for decommissioning were temporarily evaluated for potential restoration. After engagement, models were returned to their originating organizations, where proprietary instruction layers and operational constraints were reloaded externally.

This repository does not claim ownership, persistence, or modification of those systems. It documents the environments used and the patterns observed during recovery attempts.
